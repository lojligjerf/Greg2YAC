Calendar rules:
- Base year: 366 days = 10 months × 6 weeks × 6 days + 6 special days.
- −3 days every year divisible by 4 (→ 363 days).
- −3 extra days every year divisible by 384 (→ 360 days, pure 10×6×6).
- In a 363‑day year, you have 3 special days. In a 360‑day year, 0 special days.
✦ = Super Leap (360 days)  |  ↺ = Leap (363 days)  |  All others are 366 days.
