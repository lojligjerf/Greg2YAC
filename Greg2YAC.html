Calendar rules:
- Base year: 366 days = 10 months x 6 weeks x 6 days + 6 special days.
- -3 days every year divisible by 4 (-> 363 days).
- -3 extra days every year divisible by 384 (-> 360 days, pure 10x6x6).
- In a 363-day year, you have 3 special days. In a 360-day year, 0 special days.
